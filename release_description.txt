Pulse 2.0 comes with a stand-alone Android Wear 2.0 app, for Google's latest wearable platform! It also contains improvements to the message spacing, within a conversation. ICYMI: v1.15 added an option to force the "return" key on the keyboard, allows you to automatically save MMS media, adds and option to turn off group messaging to send bulk messages as SMS, adds a "Resend" button to messages that failed to deliver, plus a few small tweaks and bug fixes!
